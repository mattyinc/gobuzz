# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Go'Buzz serves wellness-minded adults in Addis Ababa who want a premium, calm, trustworthy place for sauna, cold plunge, recovery, and personal reset. Visitors arrive either exploring the club for the first time or trying to reserve a session quickly.

## Product Purpose

The site introduces Go'Buzz Wellness as a private wellness club, communicates the Move / Think / Recover method, builds confidence in the facilities, and guides visitors into booking sauna or ice bath sessions. Success means the brand feels credible and distinctive while the booking path remains clear.

## Brand Personality

Calm, intentional, premium. The voice should feel grounded and quietly confident: recovery as a serious practice, not a trend or a noisy fitness pitch.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic gym marketing, clinical spa blandness, loud supplement-brand energy, stock wellness cliches, and template SaaS landing-page patterns. Do not let the booking experience feel fragile, confusing, or overdesigned.

## Design Principles

- Let the recovery atmosphere lead with real facility imagery and tactile materials.
- Keep actions obvious: booking should always feel nearby and trustworthy.
- Pair premium restraint with warmth; the brand should feel private, not cold.
- Preserve the black, gold, and warm parchment identity already in the system.
- Treat accessibility and reduced motion as part of the calm experience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus states, and keyboard navigation. Respect reduced-motion preferences, keep copy legible on dark and light themes, and ensure booking controls remain usable on mobile.
